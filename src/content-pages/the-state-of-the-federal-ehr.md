# The State of the Federal EHR

**Plan to attend The State of the Federal EHR on May 5, from noon to 2 p.m. ET.** 

The Federal Electronic Health Record Modernization (FEHRM) office will host this virtual, biannual event to discuss the current and future state of the Federal Electronic Health Record (EHR), including a fireside chat with senior leaders from the Department of War, Department of Veterans Affairs, Department of Commerce’s National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, and the FEHRM. 

This event will be virtual via Microsoft Teams and open to the public. Additional details regarding the agenda and meeting will be distributed to registered participants prior to the event. 

If you have any questions about this event, please send an email to [Dha.ncr.peo-ipo.mbx.FEHRM-strategic-coordination@health.mil](mailto:Dha.ncr.peo-ipo.mbx.FEHRM-strategic-coordination@health.mil) and include “The State of the Federal EHR” in the subject line.
