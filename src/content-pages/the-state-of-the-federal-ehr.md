# The State of the Federal EHR

The Federal Electronic Health Record Modernization (FEHRM) office hosted The State of the Federal EHR on May 5. This virtual, biannual event—which is presented via Microsoft Teams and open to the public—included a discussion on the current and future state of the Federal Electronic Health Record (EHR) with senior leaders from the Department of War, Department of Veterans Affairs, Department of Commerce’s National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, and the FEHRM. 

[Watch The State of the Federal EHR now.](https://www.dvidshub.net/video/1005847/may-2026-state-federal-electronic-health-record)
